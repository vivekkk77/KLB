DEDICATED CAPACITY

FCL Shipping (Full Container Load)

For high-volume shipments, FCL offers dedicated container space exclusively for your cargo. This ensures maximum security, faster transit times with direct routing, and fewer handling points compared to consolidated freight.

  • 20ft, 40ft & High Cube options
  • Refrigerated (Reefer) Containers
  • Open Top & Flat Rack
  • Direct Vessel Routing

COST-EFFECTIVE FLEXIBILITY

LCL Shipping (Less than Container Load)

When you don't have enough cargo to fill a whole container, LCL allows you to share space and split costs. You pay only for the volume you utilize. We manage the consolidation and deconsolidation process seamlessly at our secure facilities.

  • Weekly Consolidated Sailings
  • Pay-per-CBM Pricing
  • Secure CFS Warehousing
  • Palletization & Shrink-wrapping
